About

I’m Justin Ly, a programmer with skills in web development, game development, database management, writing, and more. I have a deep passion for game development, and while that fire isn’t going away any time soon, I want to apply that same motivation and drive to branching into other programming disciplines. I’ve found that a lot of the same principles end up translating into good practices no matter where you are in the tech world, such as problem-solving and critical thinking skills, and the ability to work well and work fast in a team; these are qualities that I recognize within myself among my other skills. My past experiences working on teams, both in and out of tech, have helped me flex these qualities and also helped train my communication and leadership skills to anticipate, identify, and solve problems to ensure a quality experience for end users.
