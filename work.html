Games & Personal Projects

Cafe At The End of Time

"Cafe At The End of Time" is a game where you appear in a place outside of time and space without knowing how or when you got there, and there isn't much to do for you but serve coffee to other unfortunate saps that have found themselves out of time.

Originally intended to be a one-time game jam submission, I decided to spend more effort into turning this project into a more realized experience and proof-of-concept for ideas I would try in the future.

Coming soon to itch.io!

Project Tabletop RED

Project Tabletop RED is a fan-project for the tabletop roleplaying game Cyberpunk RED from R. Talsorian Games. Made in the Godot game engine, you can create and manage your character sheets through this free app.

I had noticed that similar TTRPGs like Dungeons & Dragons had a plethora of character creator apps available, but Cyberpunk RED seemed to be a little lacking in that regard, so I took it upon myself to fill the gap a little bit.

Coming soon to itch.io!
